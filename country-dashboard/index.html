Project Bubbles

oDesk serves the world

Over 250,000 businesses across the world have used oDesk to connect with contractors. We've helped people connect across oceans, mountains, and cultures. We wanted to give you a way to explore oDesk and see our marketplace from a global view. If you are feeling inspired, head over to oDesk and post a job for free or start looking for work.

Compare Activity

There are contractors from over 180 different countries on oDesk, each representing a distinct culture. This section lets you cross these boundaries and compare each country according to their own local time. Examples to get you started: G8 countries, South America, and a selection of Northern countries.

Map

Click on a country to toggle it's selection. Hover to display names.

Number of workers active during the week

A chart of the number of workers active over the course of the week. Hover to see the time interval, country name, and number of workers online.

Y-Scale

Percentage of registered workers that were active

A chart of the percentage of registered workers that were active in a country over the course of a week. Hover to see the time interval, country name, and relative number of workers online.

Legend

The countries and colors that represent them.

The map below shows the level of activity in each country. It represents the average activity for the 52 weeks of 2011. The darker the color of the country, the more active the country was on average during the current time. Hover over the country to see more information.

Watch the World Work

oDesk has created one of the first truly global labor marketplaces. With our ever growing network of contractors, clients are able to outsource any sort of work at any time of the day. This section provides a way to watch and understand the dynamics of the activity in the oDesk market over the course of a week. Start by moving your mouse across the line chart below.

Weekly Global Activity

The graph below shows the ratio of contractors working during the given interval to the total number of hours worked during the week. Hover over the chart to change the time and update the map.

Projects Galore

The oDesk marketplace has contractors who can complete almost any type of job. This section allows you to explore the types of projects completed within each country. Every major category has several sub-categories, representing a more specific type of work. Examples to get you started: Thailand, Finland, and Chile. Click on the map below to switch countries.

Map of Projects Completed

The redder the country is the greater the number of projects that have been completed in the country. Click on a country to select it for the bubble chart.

Below is a chart that packs together the sub-categories based on the number of projects completed in both the specific sub-category and major category overall. Hover over a bubble to see the full name of the sub-category and the percentage of projects completed.
